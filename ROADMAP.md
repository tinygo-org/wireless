## More Protocols

The following protocols are of interest, but still await implementation:

- APRS (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Automatic_Packet_Reporting_System)

- AX.25 (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AX.25)

- Bell modem (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell_103)

- Morse (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morse_code#Amateur_radio)

- RTTY (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Radioteletype)

- SSTV (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slow-scan_television)
